Squeeze Breakout Dynamics in Crypto
Research Note Summary

This note summarizes volatility compression and expansion behavior.

Core observations:
- Volatility compression periods often precede directional expansion.
- Breakouts with weak volume or weak momentum confirmation fail more often.
- Context matters: trend-aligned breakouts perform better than counter-trend releases.

Practical setup:
- Detect squeeze state via BB inside KC.
- Trade only post-release with positive momentum confirmation.
- Add volume-ratio condition to improve signal quality.

Risk controls:
- Use ATR-based initial stops.
- Enforce max-hold windows when expansion does not follow through.
