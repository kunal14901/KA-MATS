Bitcoin as a Relative Safe-Haven During Altcoin Stress
Research Note Summary

This note captures flight-to-quality behavior in crypto drawdowns.

Core observations:
- During broad altcoin deleveraging, BTC and ETH often decline less than high-beta alts.
- Relative strength ranking is more informative than absolute returns in stressed tape.
- Safe-haven behavior is conditional, not guaranteed.

Practical trading implications:
- In volatile or bearish regimes, prioritize BTC/ETH when cross-rank confirms relative leadership.
- Avoid defensive rotation when BTC/ETH are also weak on relative basis.
- Use wider ATR stops in stress regimes due to gap and wick risk.

Risk notes:
- Correlation spikes can still pull BTC/ETH down in systemic events.
- Position sizing should remain volatility-normalized.
