Correlation Contagion in Altcoin and DeFi Baskets
Research Note Summary

This note summarizes diversification limits in crypto stress periods.

Core observations:
- Correlations between risk-on crypto assets often rise sharply during selloffs.
- Apparent diversification in calm markets can disappear in drawdowns.
- Concentration risk increases when symbols share narrative and liquidity drivers.

Portfolio implications:
- Cap total portfolio exposure.
- Limit simultaneous positions in highly correlated sleeves.
- Favor relative-quality assets during stress regimes.

Risk controls:
- Track realized basket correlation over rolling windows.
- Tighten exposure caps when contagion risk is elevated.
