Heikin-Ashi Trend Confirmation for Crypto
Research Note Summary

This note summarizes why Heikin-Ashi filtering can reduce noise.

Core observations:
- Heikin-Ashi candle structure filters intrabar noise compared with raw candles.
- Trend resumption bars with strong body characteristics often outperform plain RSI pullback entries.
- Confirmation works best when aligned with EMA structure and trend strength.

Practical entry framework:
- Require trend regime confirmation first.
- Use HA bullish structure plus moving-average alignment.
- Add relative-strength filter to avoid weak-symbol entries.

Risk management:
- ATR-based stops are still required; HA does not remove volatility risk.
- Time-based exits prevent capital from stalling in failed resumptions.
