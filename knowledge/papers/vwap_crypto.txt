VWAP Mean Reversion in 24/7 Crypto Markets
Research Note Summary

This note summarizes VWAP deviation behavior for liquid crypto pairs.

Core observations:
- Large downside displacement from anchored VWAP often reverts in non-trending tape.
- Signals degrade sharply when broader regime is directional and persistent.
- BTC/ETH generally show cleaner VWAP behavior than thin altcoins.

Practical rules:
- Prefer ranging or mean-reverting regimes.
- Combine VWAP deviation with oversold momentum filter.
- Avoid macro-downtrend knife-catching with trend filter.

Execution guidance:
- VWAP is best used as entry context; exits still need favorable reward-to-risk.
- Stop and target distances should adapt to ATR.
