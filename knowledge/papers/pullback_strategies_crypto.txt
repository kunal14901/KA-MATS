Trend Pullback Playbook for Crypto
Research Note Summary

This note summarizes robust pullback behavior in uptrends.

Core observations:
- Pullbacks to fast/medium moving averages offer better reward-to-risk than breakout chasing.
- Quality pullbacks preserve broader trend structure and recover with expanding momentum.
- Deep pullbacks in weak trend contexts can become full regime transitions.

Practical setup filters:
- Trend regime required (ADX and MA structure aligned).
- Pullback depth constrained to avoid knife-catching.
- Relative-strength and volume context preferred.

Execution and risk:
- Place stop beyond volatility envelope, not directly at moving average touch.
- Scale position by stop distance so per-trade risk remains stable.
