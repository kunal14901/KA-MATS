Fractional Sizing in High-Volatility Crypto Systems
Research Note Summary

This note summarizes sizing discipline under high variance.

Core observations:
- Overbetting accelerates drawdown nonlinearly in volatile assets.
- Fractional risk sizing improves survivability and compounding stability.
- Portfolio-level exposure caps are as important as per-trade risk.

Practical framework:
- Define fixed risk budget per trade as a percentage of equity.
- Convert risk budget to units using stop distance.
- Apply hard caps for max position size and total exposure.

Operational notes:
- Recompute size after each equity change.
- Enforce drawdown and daily-loss circuit breakers.
